
# Admonitions

With the flag `MD_FLAG_ADMONITIONS`, MD4C enables extension for recognition of
admonitions:

```````````````````````````````` example
> [!NOTE]
> My note text.
.
<div class="admonition-note"><p class="admonition-title">note</p><p>My note text.</p>
</div>
.
--fadmonitions
````````````````````````````````

